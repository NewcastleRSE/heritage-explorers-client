Hello my name is Edward and this is my father Arthur. The year is 1825. We are in London for the day at GROSVENOR SQUARE where we have just made a morning call, visiting with our friends in the city. At the end of the day my father promised to show me the BANK OF ENGLAND. Would you like to meet us there? The first to arrive will win the game. It won’t be easy – there are so many sites to see and things to do in London.
